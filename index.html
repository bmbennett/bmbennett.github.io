All about me

Hello. Nice to meet you digitally.

I am a freelance graphic designer and Creative Director of the US based company Luxe Beat Media. I design, build and manage their digital publication, Luxe Beat Magazine. Previous to this I was the Art Editor for the Sunday Express Magazine based in London.

I am currently growing webbed limbs and have completed courses in UX Design, Programming and After Effects. I would love to hear from anyone who might offer work experience in UX and Programming.

I am experienced in art directing, photoshoots, project managing and have worked with InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator for 15 years along with many other apps.

I am also building a name for myself as a travel writer – working assignments across Europe and US. My passion for food landed me the opportunity to work with chef Katherine Frelon – designing and producing her first book, ‘Shop. Cook. Eat.’

…Oh, and I also make beer!
